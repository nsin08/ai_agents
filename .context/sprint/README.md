# Sprint Context

## Cadence

- **Sprint Duration:** 2 weeks
- **Sprint Planning:** Every other Monday, 10:00 AM
- **Sprint Review/Retro:** Every other Friday, 4:00 PM
- **Standup:** Daily, 9:00 AM (async via Slack or sync as needed)

## Naming Convention

Sprint folders follow the pattern: `sprint-{YYYY}-W{week}`

Example: `sprint-2026-W03` (weeks 3, 2026)

## Files

Each sprint should include:

| File | Purpose | Owner |
|------|---------|-------|
| `sprint-{YYYY}-W{week}/plan.md` | Sprint goals and story assignments | PM |
| `sprint-{YYYY}-W{week}/retro.md` | Lessons learned and action items | Tech Lead |
| `sprint-{YYYY}-W{week}/notes/` | Meeting notes (planning, standup, retro) | Team |

## Tracking

Stories assigned to a sprint are labeled `sprint:{YYYY}-W{week}` in GitHub.

Example: `sprint:2026-W03`

## Approval Process

1. **Plan:** PM creates sprint plan (due Sunday before sprint)
2. **Review:** Tech Lead reviews and approves
3. **Kickoff:** Monday 10 AM — team confirms capacity
4. **Retro:** Friday 4 PM — document lessons and next sprint improvements
