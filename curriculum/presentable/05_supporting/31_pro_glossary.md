# Pro Glossary

**Level**: Pro
**Scope**: Research and innovation concepts

---

## Terms

1. **Benchmark suite**: A curated set of tests for model evaluation. (Related: [Chapter 4: Research Frontiers](../04_pro/chapter_04_research_frontiers.md))
2. **Rater agreement**: Consistency between human evaluators. (Related: [Chapter 4: Research Frontiers](../04_pro/chapter_04_research_frontiers.md))
3. **A/B test**: Controlled comparison between two variants. (Related: [Chapter 4: Research Frontiers](../04_pro/chapter_04_research_frontiers.md))
4. **Counterfactual**: Alternate scenario used to test robustness. (Related: [Chapter 4: Research Frontiers](../04_pro/chapter_04_research_frontiers.md))
5. **Red teaming**: Structured adversarial testing for safety. (Related: [Chapter 4: Research Frontiers](../04_pro/chapter_04_research_frontiers.md))
6. **Adversarial prompt**: Input designed to bypass guardrails. (Related: [Chapter 3: Agentic Design Patterns](../04_pro/chapter_03_agentic_design_patterns.md))
7. **Calibration**: Alignment between confidence and correctness. (Related: [Chapter 2: Reasoning Architectures](../04_pro/chapter_02_reasoning_architectures.md))
8. **Pareto frontier**: Trade-off curve between competing objectives. (Related: [Chapter 4: Research Frontiers](../04_pro/chapter_04_research_frontiers.md))
9. **Cost curve**: Relationship between cost and quality. (Related: [Chapter 1: Advanced Frameworks](../04_pro/chapter_01_advanced_frameworks.md))
10. **Systemic bias**: Bias in outputs due to data or model design. (Related: [Chapter 4: Research Frontiers](../04_pro/chapter_04_research_frontiers.md))
11. **Preference modeling**: Learning user preferences over time. (Related: [Chapter 2: Reasoning Architectures](../04_pro/chapter_02_reasoning_architectures.md))
12. **Reward model**: Model used to score outputs in RLHF. (Related: [Chapter 4: Research Frontiers](../04_pro/chapter_04_research_frontiers.md))
13. **Evaluation harness**: Tooling that runs standardized evaluations. (Related: [Chapter 4: Research Frontiers](../04_pro/chapter_04_research_frontiers.md))
14. **Data provenance**: Traceability of data sources. (Related: [Chapter 4: Research Frontiers](../04_pro/chapter_04_research_frontiers.md))
15. **Reproducibility**: Ability to repeat results with same setup. (Related: [Chapter 4: Research Frontiers](../04_pro/chapter_04_research_frontiers.md))
16. **Drift detection**: Monitoring for performance changes over time. (Related: [Chapter 4: Research Frontiers](../04_pro/chapter_04_research_frontiers.md))
